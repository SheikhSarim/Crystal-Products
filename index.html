Each stone, whether it be a sparkling diamond, vibrant sapphire, enchanting emerald, or any other gem, adds a touch of allure and significance to the ring. Tumbled Stone

Tumbled stones are small, polished gemstones that have been smoothed and shaped through a process called tumbling. This process involves placing rough stones in a machine with abrasive grit and water, which then rotates and tumbles the stones over a period of time, gradually wearing away their rough edges and creating a smooth, polished surface.
